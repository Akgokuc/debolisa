About Us

Debolisa

Dobolisa specializes in exporting high-quality agricultural and consumer goods from India, including jaggery, Basmati rice, and spices. We import items like dates and kiwi and offer RO water production.

Our focus is on quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction, serving both domestic and international markets.
